# Branch B — Reading List

## Empirical update 2026-04-23

Branch B is now reframed around Phase 2a [[SIM-CoT]] as the auxiliary-step-decoder intervention on top of the Phase 1 V2 detach recipe. The F1-F6 battery on the Phase-1 anchor (see `.raw/experiments/inert_latent_hypothesis_tests`) **strengthens the motivating hypothesis for SIM-CoT** and gives the branch concrete numeric success criteria:

- F3 showed 7/8 latent positions decode to a fixed template (entropy <0.4 bits), with only step 3 carrying any cross-example variation (1.49 bits over a three-way digit branch). SIM-CoT's step-supervised aux decoder is exactly the intervention designed to force per-step content into those positions.
- F5 cross-example KV swap leaves accuracy unchanged (0.10→0.10, 13% text change at N=30), confirming that without an auxiliary signal the decoder treats the latent prefix as a generic routing key.
- F4 ablation floor at 25-29% accuracy loss sets a lower bound on what a successful SIM-CoT run must clear.

**Success criteria (F-test-derived).** A SIM-CoT checkpoint is "Phase-2 successful" iff: F3 entropy rises above 2 bits on multiple positions (not just step 3); F5 swap becomes non-null (>25% prediction-text change and a measurable accuracy drop under foreign-example KV); F6 σ=0.5 accuracy stays well above the current <3% collapse floor; capability moves from 0.16 toward the Qwen3-4B zero-shot 0.86 floor.

**Implementation status.** Full-LM aux (`aux_decoder_full_lm: true`) OOMs at step 1 on the GH200; **shared-`lm_head` smoke (`aux_decoder_full_lm: false`) is green** (10/10 steps, 22.5 GB peak, 2026-04-24 log). The shared-head fallback is the working path. If shared-head trains to completion but fails to move F3/F5/F6, that tells us the aux signal must carry full LM capacity — a concrete hypothesis for Branch B to pressure-test.

## Empirical update 2026-04-24

SIM-CoT shared-head smoke confirmed (bs=1/ga=1, 10/10 steps, 48 s, **22.5 GB peak**) while LT-Tuning co-ran at 38.2 GB on the same GH200. Full-LM variant still OOM-blocked at Qwen3-4B. Go-path: wait for LT-Tuning to release the GPU, retry full-LM SIM-CoT on idle, fall back to shared-head if that also OOMs. Shared-head is not safe to co-run with LT-Tuning at bs=2/ga=4 (combined peak too close to the 96 GB budget).
