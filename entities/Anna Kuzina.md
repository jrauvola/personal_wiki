# Anna Kuzina

First author of [[KaVa]] (*Latent Reasoning via Compressed KV-Cache Distillation*, arXiv:2510.02312, Oct 2025). Affiliated with **Qualcomm AI Research** in collaboration with IDEAS NCBR / IPPT PAN.

## Contributions tracked in this vault

- **KaVa** — introduced KV-cache distillation as a per-step supervision signal for latent reasoning students, closing the CODI/COCONUT supervision gap via R-KV eviction + an L_KV matching loss coupled with PCCoT parallel decoding.
