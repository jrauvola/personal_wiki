# Bernhard Schölkopf

Director at MPI for Intelligent Systems (Tübingen); Amazon Scholar; senior author across the causal representation learning literature.

## Key contributions (this vault)

- **Towards Causal Representation Learning** (Schölkopf, Locatello, Bauer, Ke, Kalchbrenner, Goyal, Bengio 2021) — canonical review of causal representation learning. Frames the Independent Causal Mechanisms principle, sparse mechanism shift, and the ingredients needed for causal disentanglement beyond the Locatello 2019 impossibility result.
- Foundational work on Independent Causal Mechanisms (Peters, Janzing, Schölkopf 2017, *Elements of Causal Inference*).

See [[Causal Disentanglement]] for the integrated framework.
