Anthropic researcher; first author of "Toy Models of Superposition" (2022), which formalized the superposition hypothesis for neural networks.

## Key contributions (this vault)

- **Toy Models of Superposition** (2022) — formal framework for polysemanticity, phase-change, uniform-polytope geometry. See [[Toy Models of Superposition]].

Part of Anthropic's interpretability / circuits team alongside Christopher Olah, Catherine Olsson, Tristan Hume, and others.
